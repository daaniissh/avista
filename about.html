About

Al Bayan International Company is newly developed establishment with an objective to invest in both industrial and commercial business sectors taking a positive advantage from the fast growing economy and business opportunities in the Saudi region specially in the gas/oil, power energy and petrochemical sectors.

Our company's goal is to team up with international companies to introduce specialized engineered services, well trained and skilled personal and establish localized service support to the industrial sector.

We are an organization that is 100% Saudi Based and owned by Partners, Encouraged by the Fast Growing Business in the Industrial Sector specially in Industrial Cities G Saudi Aramco.work site.Our wide Experience in the Fields of Manufacturing ,Industrial Maintenance G Project Areas has encouraged and supported the owners to establish the company.
